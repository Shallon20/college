Who We Are

Ushindi College was founded in 2002 with a clear vision: to empower students through transformative education that prepares them for real-world challenges. From its humble beginnings as a small technical institute, Ushindi has grown into a vibrant institution known for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development.

Located in the heart of Kenya, the college serves a diverse student body across disciplines such as technology, business, health sciences, and education. Over the years, we have forged strong partnerships with industry leaders and global institutions to ensure our students receive relevant, world-class training.

At Ushindi, our focus goes beyond academics. We nurture leadership, creativity, and ethical responsibility cultivating graduates who are not only skilled professionals but also changemakers in their communities. With a legacy of thousands of alumni making a difference globally, Ushindi College continues to rise as a beacon of opportunity and success.
